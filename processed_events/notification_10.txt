Description:
This Student Development Programme (SDP) aims to provide hands-on practical methods for designing Smart IoT Devices. Participants will learn about Microprocessor and Microcontroller, Computer Organization and Architecture, Embedded C & Assembly Language. The program offers practical exposure to IoT, focusing on designing real-world products and understanding applications of IoT and embedded systems. It also includes guidance on product design and mini-projects. Attendees will have the opportunity to write their own research papers and publish them. Selected students may also receive a paid internship at NSRIT Start-UPS immediately.

Topics: Introduction to IoT and Its Architecture, Introduction to Embedded C & Assembly, Microprocessor and Microcontroller, IoT and its Applications Development, IoT Product Design and Mini Project, Design of Smart IoT Devices, Computer Organization and Architecture, Real-world applications of IoT and embedded systems
